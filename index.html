How it works

From raw statements to attorney-ready report

Three steps. No manual data entry. No spreadsheets. No templates to configure.

1

Upload your statements

PDF or CSV from any bank or credit union. LedgerIQ's AI parser handles the formatting automatically — no templates needed.

2

AI extracts and categorizes

Transactions are extracted, deduplicated, and mapped to B22C means test categories in seconds.

3

Export the audit report

A structured, attorney-ready report with full reconciliation, anomaly flags, and monthly breakdowns.

Features

Built for accuracy and privacy

Every design decision was made with the end user — and their attorney — in mind.

Universal bank support

Works with any bank statement format. AI extraction means no manual template configuration.

B22C means test mapping

Transactions automatically categorized and mapped to the correct means test fields.

Local encrypted processing

Financial data is processed and stored locally. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Anomaly and duplicate detection

Flags suspicious transactions, large transfers, and duplicate entries before export.

Monthly trend analysis

Month-over-month spending breakdowns with fixed vs variable categorization.

One-click Windows installer

No Python, no command line. Download, install, and run — that's it.
